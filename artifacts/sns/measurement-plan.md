# SNS Measurement Plan

## Events

- `survey_landing_view`
- `survey_started`
- `survey_completed`
- `result_viewed`
- `beta_optin`
- `interview_optin`
- `pilot_requested`

## Allowed Properties

- `persona`
- `survey_version`
- `scoring_version`
- `utm_source`
- `utm_medium`
- `utm_campaign`
- `utm_content`
- `question_count`
- `completion_time_band`
- `result_band`

## Excluded Data

- Email
- Company name
- Memo or free text
- Individual answers
- Raw URL parameters beyond UTM
- Third-party advertising identifiers

## Review Cadence

Review by channel and persona after the first 24 hours, 72 hours, and 7 days. Do not change more than one major variable at a time.
