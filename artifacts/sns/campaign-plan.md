# AgentProof SNS Survey Campaign Plan

- Prepared at: 2026-06-21
- Production URL: https://agentproofkr.github.io/
- Campaign: `ai_readiness_launch`
- Medium: `organic_social`
- Publishing state: assets prepared; authenticated SNS publishing access not available in this Codex session.

## Goal

Bring qualified practitioners, leaders, and security/policy owners into the role-specific AI self-check without collecting email before the result.

## Personas

| Persona | Landing URL | Core problem |
|---|---|---|
| Practitioner | `/survey/practitioner/` | AI 답변을 어디까지 확인하고 업무에 써도 되는지 |
| Leader | `/survey/leader/` | 어떤 업무부터 AI를 도입해야 효과와 위험을 설명할 수 있는지 |
| Security | `/survey/security/` | 직원이 어떤 AI에 어떤 정보를 넣는지, 어떤 기준과 기록이 필요한지 |

## Publishing Order

1. LinkedIn: longer problem-led post, one persona per day.
2. Threads: shorter question-led version, same UTM channel.
3. Instagram: carousel caption with profile/link-sticker URL.
4. Naver Blog or community: explain who should take the check and what not to submit.

## Guardrails

- Do not claim certification, compliance, security approval, or legal advice.
- Do not say posts were published until real URLs and timestamps are recorded.
- Do not add Meta Pixel, retargeting, or third-party survey-answer tracking.
- Do not put email, company, or answers in URLs.
- Keep `utm_source` to the channel only.

## Launch Blocker

Authenticated SNS publishing access and approved publishing APIs are not available in this session. Manual publishing is required unless account access is granted.
